Closing the Loop

Tina Huang

When Grayson Welo was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes, she found herself saddled with a bag of devices to monitor and support her pancreatic functions. However, the introduction of the RileyLink, which serves as a translator between her insulin pump and smartphone, has allowed her to leave the bag at home. Welo discusses how the RileyLink has impacted her life as a diabetic and its implications for the future of biotechnology.

The RileyLink connects Grayson Welo's smartphone to her insulin pump, allowing her to monitor and adjust her insulin levels through the Loop application. Photo taken by Grayson Welo
